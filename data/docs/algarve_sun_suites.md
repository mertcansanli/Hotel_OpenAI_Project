# Algarve Sun Suites

Location: Albufeira, Algarve, Portugal  
Hotel type: Beach hotel  
Suitable for: Couples, families, summer holidays

## Pet Policy

Algarve Sun Suites accepts pets only in selected rooms.

Rules:
- Dogs up to 15 kg are accepted.
- Cats are not accepted.
- A maximum of 2 dogs is allowed per room.
- Pets are allowed only in Garden View rooms.
- Pets are not allowed in the pool area or restaurant.
- The pet fee is 15 EUR per pet per night.

Guests must contact the hotel before booking to confirm pet-friendly room availability.

## Children Policy

Children are welcome.

Rules:
- Children under 2 years old stay for free.
- Baby cots are available on request.
- Extra beds are not available.
- The hotel has a children's pool.
- The hotel does not provide babysitting services.

## Breakfast

Breakfast is optional and costs 14 EUR per adult per day.

Children between 3 and 10 years old pay 7 EUR for breakfast.

## Check-in and Check-out

Check-in starts at 15:00.  
Check-out is until 10:30.

## Parking

Free public parking is available near the hotel.

The hotel does not guarantee parking spaces.

## Cancellation Policy

Standard bookings can be cancelled free of charge until 7 days before arrival.

During July and August, cancellations must be made at least 14 days before arrival.

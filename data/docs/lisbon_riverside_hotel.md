# Lisbon Riverside Hotel

Location: Lisbon, Portugal  
Hotel type: City hotel  
Suitable for: Couples, business travelers, short city breaks

## Pet Policy

Lisbon Riverside Hotel allows small pets up to 10 kg.

Rules:
- Only dogs and cats are accepted.
- A maximum of 1 pet is allowed per room.
- Pets cannot be left alone in the room.
- Pets are not allowed in the breakfast area, spa, or gym.
- There is an extra cleaning fee of 20 EUR per night.

Guests must inform the hotel before arrival if they are bringing a pet.

## Children Policy

Children of all ages are welcome.

Rules:
- Children under 3 years old can stay for free when using existing beds.
- Baby cots are available on request and are free of charge.
- Extra beds are available only in Superior Rooms and Suites.
- Children under 12 years old receive a discounted breakfast price.

## Breakfast

Breakfast is served from 07:00 to 10:30.

Breakfast is included only in "Bed & Breakfast" and "Flexible Breakfast Included" rates.

## Check-in and Check-out

Check-in starts at 15:00.  
Check-out is until 11:00.

Early check-in depends on room availability.

## Parking

Private parking is available for 18 EUR per day.

Parking reservation is recommended because spaces are limited.

## Cancellation Policy

Flexible bookings can be cancelled free of charge until 24 hours before arrival.

Non-refundable bookings cannot be cancelled or refunded.

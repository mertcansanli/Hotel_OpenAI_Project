# Porto Family Resort

Location: Porto, Portugal  
Hotel type: Family resort  
Suitable for: Families with children, long stays, holiday trips

## Pet Policy

Porto Family Resort does not accept pets.

Exception:
- Certified assistance dogs are accepted without an extra fee.

Guests traveling with an assistance dog should notify the hotel before arrival.

## Children Policy

Children of all ages are welcome.

Family-friendly services:
- Kids club for children between 4 and 12 years old.
- Outdoor playground.
- Family rooms for up to 4 guests.
- Baby cots are available for free.
- High chairs are available in the restaurant.

Children under 6 years old can stay for free when sharing a room with parents.

## Breakfast

Breakfast is included in all room rates.

Breakfast is served from 07:30 to 11:00.

## Check-in and Check-out

Check-in starts at 16:00.  
Check-out is until 12:00.

## Parking

Outdoor parking is free for hotel guests.

## Cancellation Policy

Flexible bookings can be cancelled free of charge until 48 hours before arrival.

Late cancellations are charged for the first night.

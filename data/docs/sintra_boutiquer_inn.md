# Sintra Boutique Inn

Location: Sintra, Portugal  
Hotel type: Small boutique guesthouse  
Suitable for: Couples, quiet stays, cultural trips

## Pet Policy

Sintra Boutique Inn does not accept pets.

No pets are allowed in rooms or common areas.

Certified guide dogs are accepted when required by law.

## Children Policy

Sintra Boutique Inn accepts children aged 12 and above.

The property is not recommended for younger children because:
- There are many stairs.
- Rooms are small.
- There is no elevator.
- Baby cots are not available.
- Extra beds are not available.

Guests under 18 must stay with an adult.

## Breakfast

Continental breakfast is included in all bookings.

Breakfast is served from 08:00 to 10:00.

## Check-in and Check-out

Check-in is from 14:00 to 20:00.  
Check-out is until 11:00.

Late check-in after 20:00 must be requested in advance.

## Parking

The hotel does not have private parking.

Public street parking is available nearby but cannot be reserved.

## Cancellation Policy

Bookings can be cancelled free of charge until 72 hours before arrival.

No-shows are charged the full amount of the first night.
